Here are the reasons for the clusters. 
They are not directly innovation based but just different goals. These are what we are looking for.
Large Banks:

Securities Shifters: Moving away from loans into securities
Loan Growth Focus: Aggressive loan portfolio expansion
Quality Improvers: Best credit quality improvement (chargeoff reduction)
Digital Deposit Leaders: Highest digital banking adoption (NIB deposits)

Medium Banks:

Profitability Leaders: Strong ROE/ROA gains, restructuring capital
Branch Consolidators: Reducing physical footprint (occupancy costs)
Steady Performers: The "typical" medium bank trajectory
High Growth Stars: Exceptional profitability growth

Small Banks:

Digital Transformers: Radical shift to non-branch revenue streams
Traditional Majority: Maintaining traditional banking model
Capital Constrained: Struggling with capital levels
